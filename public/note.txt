Image Upload & Sale – Upload, preview, categorize, and sell images
Video Download System – Upload, stream, and sell videos
Subscription System – Tiered plans with limited or unlimited downloads

Licensing Options – Personal, commercial, extended
User Dashboard – Purchases, downloads, subscription status
Ratings & Reviews – Buyers can rate content
Payment Gateway – Stripe, PayPal, or local options
Creator Monetization – Revenue tracking and payouts

Optional/Nice-to-Have:
Watermark Previews & DRM – Protect content from piracy
Analytics – Popular content, top creators
Affiliate/Referral System – Encourage referrals
